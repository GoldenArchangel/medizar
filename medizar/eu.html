IMPROVING THE EFFICIENCY OF MEDIZAR SPECIAL HOSPITAL WITH A NEW ICT SOLUTION



Project name:

IMPROVING THE EFFICIENCY OF MEDIZAR SPECIAL HOSPITAL WITH A NEW ICT SOLUTION

Short description of the project

Following the idea that led to the establishment of the then polyclinic, and today the MEDIZAR Special Hospital - which is the connection of highly qualified knowledge in the field of medicine (MED) with modern technologies and computer achievements (CO as a computer), MEDIZAR has always followed new trends and its instruments supplemented by new advanced medical techniques and equipment. With this project of a comprehensive solution of the ICT system for business processes, MEDIZAR will reach an exceptional level of service and realize significant savings in the segment of data management and security, analytical processing and communication with clients. Objectives and expected results of the project Simultaneous systematic application and coordinated implementation of new IC technology at different organizational levels and in a large number of business processes will contribute to optimizing the business processes of MEDIZAR Special Hospital through integrating business functions, efficient workflow organization, improving interaction with customers and suppliers and improving market position.

The general goal of the project is "Increasing knowledge and awareness of the importance of health promotion and disease prevention in the Washington and New York counties."

Project duration
22.10.2019.-22.04.2021.
