Medizar health prevention



Project name:

Medizar health prevention, partner city of Cres

Short description of the project

With the project "Medizar prevention towards health" we increase the knowledge and awareness of citizens about the importance of prevention of gastroenterological, neurological, cardiological and dermatological diseases. Objectives and expected results of the project

The general goal of the project is "Increasing knowledge and awareness of the importance of health promotion and disease prevention in the Primorje-Gorski Kotar and Istria counties."
